ARC. ABDULLAHI SULEIMAN
CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER

As the Chief Executive Officer of Pirotti Group Ltd, Arc. Abdullahi Suleiman MNIA is an architect by design and an entrepreneur by instinct. His journey is a tapestry woven with rich experiences and a relentless drive for innovation within the architectural landscape.

With a Master of Science (MSc.) degree in Architecture from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, Abdullahi brings forth over 12 years of profound expertise in architectural design, construction, regulatory compliance, and project management. His diverse background spans both public and private sectors, honing his skills across various facets of the industry.
A distinguished member of the Nigerian Institute of Architects and licensed by the Architects Registration Council of Nigeria (ARCON), Abdullahi's influence extends beyond Pirotti Projects Ltd. He serves as a Non-Executive Director on the board of NGX Real Estate Limited (NGX RelCo), contributing invaluable insights and shaping the industry landscape.

His tenure at the Federal Ministry of Housing and Urban Development fortified his comprehensive understanding of the housing sector. Beyond his professional endeavors, Abdullahi's fervor for exploring diverse cultures through travel and his affinity for nature complement his holistic approach to life. At the helm of Pirotti Group Ltd., Abdullahi orchestrates a vision that intertwines creativity, sustainability, and pioneering leadership. His commitment to excellence and transformative solutions is the guiding force propelling the company toward new heights of innovation and success.

SAMEER YESHWANTH
CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

Sameer Yeshwanth MBA

Sameer is an alumnus of the University of Maiduguri and the Moddibo Adama University of Technology from where he bagged his MBA from. He has a 25-year experience in Business Development, Education Management, Project Management, Strategic Management, Investments and Partnerships, SME’s, New Business Set up, Business Consulting, Sales, Construction, Hotels, Communications and PR, Branding, International Trade and Diplomacy. He has worked for a Coca Cola and Nestle Franchise, was a 3-time Director at the American University of Nigeria. He has served as Managing Director/CEO, General Manager/COO, Executive Director, Consultant to some State Governments and was also a Project Director for the upgrade for Zaranda Hotel in Bauchi in 2009 for the Junior World Cup, where he got accolades from FIFA LOC and the Nigerian Presidency.

Sameer is an ardent fan of the Chelsea Football Club.

FLORENCE DAVIDS
HEAD OF CORPORATE SERVICES

Florence holds a bachelor's Degree in Accounting from - the University of Calabar, an Advanced Diploma in Human Resources Management - from London Academy Business School, an Associate member of the Chartered Institute of Economists of Nigeria, a student member of the Chartered Institute of Personnel Management of Nigeria, and is a Certified British Project Management Professional [Project Monitoring, Evaluation, and Control] PMEC from the Project Management College United Kingdom, currently an MBA student of Ahmadu Bello University in Human Resources Specialization.

With deep administrative experience and Human Capital Management Skills, she has been able to scale through the professional ladder with almost ten (10) years of professional experience and has a track record of managing over 200 employees cumulatively.

Before her appointment with Pirotti Projects Limited as Head of Corporate Services, she served as Lead Consultant - People and Culture with Fiombas Nigeria Limited, she invested her professionalism in people management with Jamub Group of Companies, as a Human Resources Generalist, with Beconomics Consulting Limited as a Consulting Human Resources Analyst, and with Mission Alert International specializing in Skills Acquisition for the proper reintegration of combatants into the society.

Her expertise spans Talent Acquisition, Organizational Structure, Human Capital Management, Payroll, Strategic Human Resources, Office Administration, Policy Document Development and implementation, and Administration.

She is result-oriented and has a positive attitude towards work, with a mindset of serving and representing her country in a large capacity.
